FAQs - Library Management System



Frequently Asked Questions



Getting Started

1. How do I add new student to borrow a book?
To add new student, click on the 'Manage Student' at the navigation bar. Then, click Add New Student. Just fill the form and click 'Add Student' button.

2. Where can I manage book?
To mange Book, just navigate to the Manage Book section at the navigation bar. You can Add, Edit, and Delete the Book here.


Using the Library

3. What to do if the book is not available?
If the book is not available, you can simply edit the Book Status in Manage Book page. Just click Edit and change the Book Status from 'Available' to 'Not Available'

4. What to do when the new stock of book arrived ?
When the stock of books arrived, you need to update the Books. Just go to Manage Book and Add New Book. Fill in the details as asked.

5. What to do when student return the book?
After student return the book, check the list at the Dashboard, then go to the Manage Student and Delete the student from the list.


Troubleshooting

6. I forgot my password. What do I do?
If you forgot your password, click on 'Forgot Password' on the login page. Enter your email address and follow the prompts to reset your password.

7. I can't find the book I'm looking for. What can I do?
If you can't find a book, it may be currently borrowed by another user or not available in our library. You can try again later, or request for the book to be added to our collection.
